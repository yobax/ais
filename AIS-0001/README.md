# AI Identity Standard (AIS)

> **A technical standard for AI identity representation.**

AI Identity Standard (AIS) is an open research project that defines how
Artificial Intelligence represents people, organizations, and
communities.

AIS focuses on the process by which AI forms identity from evidence
rather than simply storing profile data. The project proposes a common
terminology, conceptual models, and technical specifications for
identity representation in the age of AI.

## Objectives

-   Define a common terminology for AI identity representation.
-   Standardize the relationship between Person, Purpose, Evidence,
    Inference, Representation, and Identity.
-   Establish practical methods for sharing identity with AI systems.
-   Improve the quality, transparency, and trustworthiness of
    AI-generated representations.
-   Build an open standard that can evolve through public discussion and
    collaboration.
